About Me

Previously, I've studied Anthropology at the University of Toronto and Music and Audio Engineering at Portland Community College. I've lived many places in my life, including:

  • New Jersey
  • Toronto
  • Florida
  • Virginia
  • Turkmenistan
  • Washington, DC

I've been living in Portland since 2012.

I was in Peace Corps Turkmenistan from 2007-2010, teaching English in a small desert town called Kara-Kala. Since returning, I've worked as a teacher, in the hospitality industry, and most recently in the property management industry. The latter has given me valuable insight into project management, running a budget, and interacting with a Board of Directors, however it is ultimately an unsatisfying career field, and I am looking to transition.

I'd love to apply new skills learned in the field of programming and web development towards my interests in music and art.
